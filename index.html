About Lago Coffee Co

Lago Coffee Co is a specialty coffee shop in Echo Park, Los Angeles, located on Sunset Boulevard steps from Echo Park Lake. We serve single-origin pour-overs, craft espresso drinks, and fresh pastries from local bakeries in a relaxed, laptop-friendly space with free WiFi and a dog-friendly patio.

Our beans are sourced directly from small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Kenya, then roasted locally in small batches for peak flavor. Popular drinks include our Ethiopian Yirgacheffe pour-over, oat milk latte, and house-made horchata cold brew. With a 4.8-star rating from over 120 reviews, Lago has become a go-to neighborhood spot for Echo Park locals, remote workers, and weekend visitors since opening in 2023. Open daily — weekdays 7am to 6pm, weekends 8am to 6pm.
